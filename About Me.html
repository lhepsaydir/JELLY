ABOUT ME

I am a 500 hour trained yoga teacher under the International Yoga Alliance. I completed my first 200 hours in Hatha Yoga in Rishikesh, India. I then continued my studies a year later in Goa completing my 300 hours in Yin and Yang Multi style Yoga, with a focus on Ayurveda and the 5 elements.

I have praticed yoga for 7 years and could not imagine my life without it. Yoga has healed me in ways I didn't think possible, and so I became a teacher to share this gift with others.


MISSION

To provide the community with affordable and healing sessions.


YOGA STYLE

I teach Hatha and Vinyasa flow, as well as more Yinish classes including Yoga Nidra. I love to incorporate movement, art and themes into my classes. The themes can include the 5 elements, moon cycles, Taosim and even breaking patterns in our minds.

I have taught group classes in India and the United Kingdom, aswell as private classes at home. I have also taught at DASH in the refuge.
