Private Classes

If you are looking to deepen your yoga practice with classes designed specifically for you, I offer one on one sessions both face to face and online. Each class will incorporate what you wish from your yoga practice alongside my knowledge and experience as a multi-style Yin and Yang teacher.

If you have any health conditions or concerns, I will design each session around this so you can get the most out of your practice. Private sessions are magical for those who want to build confidence in their practice before entering the class environment. As well as those who progress best from direct and personal teaching.

I look forward to deepening your practice with you. Connect with me below to organise your personalised class or if you have any questions.
