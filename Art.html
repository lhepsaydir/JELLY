PROJECTS

After completing my Philosophy degree, I worked for the DASH charity (affliated with Womans Aid) for over a year as an outreach Worker. It was during this experience I realised just how much yoga can help those in difficult situations.

I set up classes at the DASH charity where I would teach a weekly class in refuge for women who had to leave their homes and families due to domestic abuse. I would lead shakti circles aswell as meditation sessions for the survivors, and could see just how much yoga helped in their recovery.
