ETHOS

The Prana Priestess was founded with the intention to make yoga accessible to all. In western society, we are fed an idea of what it means to be a Yogi; believing that we must look a certain way or be able to achieve a certain asana to practice yoga. When in reality, it is simply presence. To be conscious of our existence both on and off the mat.

As both a student and teacher of yoga, Bronagh has experienced its healing force firsthand. This catalysed Bronagh's path as a teacher, leading her to find a style of teaching to guide each student on their journey towards the Atma. To help overcome societal conditioning to make room for personal growth.

The Prana Preistess weaves this intent into every class creatively to take each student, no matter their history, on a healing journey towards consciousness.

ॐ

The meaning behind 'The Prana Preistess'

Prana has many different meanings, one of which being the energy of consciousness & creation, which in yogic practice is also known as the breath. The Priestess comes from the archetype in Tarot 'The High Priestess'. This archetype represents inner knowledge, intuition, and spirituality. It also represents balance in opposition such as darkness and light, and Yin & Yang.

Bronagh's journey began with Tarot at the age of 13. After sharing stories of her intuition with a wise woman, she was gifted her first deck. Her knowledge of symbology and her connection to her intuition is channeled into each class. Thus 'The Prana Priestess' stands for the infusion of traditional aspects of yoga such as asana, meditation, and philosophy, with more mystical elements such as mythology, ritual, and uncovering knowledge in the unconscious mind.

Bronagh is trained in Yin and Yang Multi-style Yoga, 5 Element Yoga, Nidra, and Traditional Hatha. In each class, you will be guided on a themed journey to connect with the Atma (true essence).
