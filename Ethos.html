ETHOS

The Prana Preistess was founded with the intention to make yoga accessible to all. In western society, we are fed an ideal of what it means to be a Yogi; believing that we must look a certain way or be able to achieve a certain asana to practice yoga. When in reality, it is simply presence. To be conscious with our existence both on and off the mat.

As a both a student and teacher of yoga, Bronagh has experienced its healing force firsthand. This catalysed Bronagh's path as a teacher, leading her to find a style of teaching to guide each student on their own journey towards the Atma. To help overcome societal conditioning to make room for personal growth.

The Prana Preistess weaves this intent into each and every class creatively to take each and every student, no matter their history, on a healing journey towards conciousness.

The meaning behind 'The Prana Preistess'

Prana has many different meanings, one of which being the energy of conciousness & creation, which in yogic practice is also known as the breath. The Preistess comes from the archaetype in Tarot 'The High Preistess'. This archaetype represents inner knowlege, intuition and spirituality. It also represents balence in opposition such as the masculine and feminine, and Yin & Yang. Thus 'The Prana Preistess' stands for the infusion of traditional aspects of yoga such as asana, meditation and philosophy, with more mystical elements such as knowlege in the unconicous mind mythology and ritual.
